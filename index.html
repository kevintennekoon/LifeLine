A Social Problem

The current generation are constantly on their smartphones, it just so happens that 80 percent of the Norwegian population owns a smartphone. 20 to 30 percent will experience a depression that may feel heavy and hurtful one or several times during their lifetime. Furthermore, we look at the statistics obtained from the Ministry of Children, Youth and Family Affairs saying that 40 percent of the Norwegian youth struggling with mental health. Symptoms of poor mental health can be:

  • Sleep difficulties
  • Concentration difficulties
  • Increased or reduced appetite
  • Feelings of guilt
  • Suicidal thoughts
